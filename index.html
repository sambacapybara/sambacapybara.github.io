📖 Samba's Story

Samba, a brave nine-month-old capybara, escaped from Marwell Zoo near Winchester and has been on an incredible journey ever since.

For nearly two weeks, search teams have used thermal drones, specialist sniffer dogs, and community volunteers to try to locate her.

Her sister Tango was found quickly, but Samba — now known as "The Runner" — continues to explore freely.

Sightings have been reported in Owslebury, where locals, schoolchildren, and animal lovers are all helping in the search.

The zoo has promised not to give up. Similar events in the past took months, and Samba could still be safely out there.

If you see Samba, report it immediately. Every sighting matters.
